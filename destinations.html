Skardu Destination Guide

Places to Visit

    You can visit the following beautiful Places:

  • Shangrila Resort: Also known as "Heaven on Earth," Shangrila is a picturesque resort with a beautiful garden and a serene lake, often referred to as "Shangrila Lake."
  • Deosai National Park: This is a high-altitude plateau known as the "Land of Giants." It's a great place for wildlife enthusiasts, with opportunities to see Himalayan brown bears and various bird species.
  • Skardu Fort: Visit this historic fort to learn about the region's history and enjoy panoramic views of Skardu and the surrounding valleys.
  • Baltoro Glacier: This is one of the largest glaciers outside the polar regions and the gateway to some of the world's highest peaks.
  • Cold Desert Skardu: Also called the "Katpana Desert," this is a unique desert in a high-altitude region, known for its sand dunes and beautiful sunsets.
  • Satpara Lake: A clear and blue glacial lake located near Skardu, where you can enjoy boating and picnicking.

Things to Do

    You can also do the folling things in beautiful valley of skardu:

  • Trekking and Hiking: Skardu Valley is a paradise for trekkers and hikers. You can embark on various trekking expeditions to explore the stunning landscapes of the Karakoram Range. Popular treks include the K2 Base Camp trek, Gondogoro La trek, and the trek to the Fairy Meadows.
  • Mountain Climbing: Skardu Valley is a dream destination for mountaineers. It offers challenging peaks like K2, Gasherbrum I and II, Broad Peak, and more. Climbers from around the world come here for high-altitude mountaineering.
  • Photography: The landscapes in Skardu are a photographer's dream. Capture the stunning vistas, alpine meadows, glaciers, and mountain peaks.

Local Tips

    You can try these local food:
  • Chapshuro: This is a local favorite and a must-try. Chapshuro is a type of filled bread resembling a calzone. It's typically stuffed with minced meat (usually mutton or yak), green chilies, and a blend of spices, then baked or pan-fried.
  • Sheermal: This is a sweet, saffron-flavored bread that is typically served as a dessert or with tea.
  • Dried Fruits and Nuts: Skardu is known for its production of high-quality dried fruits and nuts, including apricots, almonds, and walnuts. These make for excellent snacks or souvenirs.
  • Balti Pilaf: A rice dish cooked with various spices and mixed with meat, vegetables, and sometimes dried fruits, creating a flavorful one-pot meal.
